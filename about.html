ABOUT SOLMIDE

25+ Years of Building Excellence in Nigeria

Our Story

Founded in the early 2000s, Solmide Concerns Limited began as a specialized aluminum fabrication workshop in Ogun State, Nigeria. What started with crafting quality aluminum windows and doors for local homes and businesses has grown into a comprehensive construction and general contracting company serving clients across Nigeria.

Our founder's vision was simple: "Build it right, build it once." This philosophy guided our early years and continues to define our approach today. We understood that in the Nigerian construction industry, quality and durability weren't just preferences—they were necessities.

From those humble beginnings, we've expanded our expertise while maintaining our core commitment to craftsmanship. Today, we're proud to be CAC Registered (RC 8540703) and have built a reputation for structures that don't just meet standards, but set them.
